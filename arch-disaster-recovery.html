Final Recommendation: Active-Active Regional Failover

The Active-Active model eliminates the need for idle standby infrastructure, enables fully automated regional failover within minutes, and continuously validates system resilience through real production traffic.

Key Advantages

  • Automated Failover via Route 53: Health checks redirect traffic within minutes, achieving an RTO of < 5 mins without human intervention.
  • Continuous Validation: All regions serve live traffic. Failover paths are always "warm," removing the risk of an untested standby environment.
  • Elastic Scaling: Instead of pre-paying for idle servers, we use Auto Scaling to expand healthy regions only when needed.

Trade-offs & Mitigation

While performance may briefly dip during a regional scale-up event, this is an accepted trade-off for the massive gains in cost efficiency and operational simplicity. To mitigate systemic failures, we use staged CI/CD deployments and environment isolation.
