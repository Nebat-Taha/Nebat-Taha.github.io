Disaster Recovery: Alternatives & Recommendations

Evaluating RTO, RPO, and cost-efficiency for mission-critical cloud resilience.

In high-stakes industries like banking or healthcare, Disaster Recovery (DR) isn't just a technical backup plan—it's a core business requirement. This analysis explores the shift from traditional passive models to modern, automated resilience.

01. The Alternatives

Model Logic RTO (Recovery Time) Cost
Active-Passive 1:1 Mapping (Idle backup) 30-60 min Very High
Pilot Light Minimal standby footprint 10-15 min High
Active-Active Mutual Regional Failover 2-4 min Optimized
